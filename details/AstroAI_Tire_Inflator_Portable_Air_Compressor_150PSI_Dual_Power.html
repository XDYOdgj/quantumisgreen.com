AstroAI Tire Inflator: Your All-in-One Inflation Solution

Whether you're on a long drive, at home working on a project, or prepping for an outdoor adventure, the AstroAI Tire Inflator is the perfect tool to have on hand. Its dual power options allow you to use it anywhere—inside your vehicle or at home—with the convenience of both 12V DC and 110V AC compatibility.

Designed for reliability and power, this inflator features two inflation modes: high-pressure for car tires and bicycle tires, and low-pressure for air mattresses and inflatable rafts. It delivers up to 150 PSI for a secure, safe ride and can achieve fast inflation speeds of 350 L/min, making it ideal for quick setup in emergencies.

With a built-in LED light and a clear pressure display screen, you can easily monitor and adjust pressure in both daylight and low-light conditions. The smart auto shut-off mechanism ensures you never overinflate, protecting your tires and maintaining optimal performance. Additionally, its power cut memory function keeps your last settings ready, so you save time and effort with every use.

This compact and durable unit is designed for easy storage in your glove compartment or tool box, making it a convenient addition to your gear. Included with the device are dual nozzles and adapters that expand its functionality to handle a wide variety of inflatables, from tires to rafts and beyond. The AstroAI Tire Inflator is a must-have for car owners, adventurers, and anyone who values efficiency and versatility in their tools.
